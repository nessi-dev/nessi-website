Frequently Asked Questions

How does the free trial work?

You can try all Pro features free for 1 month. No credit card required. The trial is limited to one per machine.

What happens when my trial ends?

When your trial ends, you'll automatically revert to the Community Edition. Your data and configurations will remain intact, but premium features will be disabled.

Is the license management system open source?

Yes, the license management code itself is open-source and transparent, allowing for community review and contributions. The anti-tampering measures only restrict access to premium features but do not obscure the implementation.

How do I get pricing for the Pro Edition?

Please contact our sales team for custom pricing based on your organization's needs. We offer flexible plans for teams of all sizes.
